Assignment 1

The task of this assignment is to reconstruct Un coup de dés jamais n’abolira le hasard (translated into English)

Levels 1-5 each contain different levels of restrictions

Level 1: Only p,br, and nbsp

Level 2: Previous levels plus div, span, h#, strong, em, ol/ul/li, and a

Level 3: Previous levels plus padding, border, margin, position, display, float, top/right/left/bottom, height, and width

Level 4: Previous levels plus font-style, font-weight, font-size, font-family, text-align, line-height, letter-spacing, and text-transformation

Level 5: Literally anything except img, iframe, and linking sites with a
